About Avance

We were a learning company first. That's exactly why our AI work sticks.

Avance Learning Solutions is an AI Operations & Workflow Architecture consulting firm — built on more than fifteen years of experience helping organizations get their people to actually change how they work.

Our Story

We've spent 15+ years solving the hard part of transformation: getting people to actually change.

Long before "AI adoption" was a line item on a strategy deck, Avance was in the trenches of enterprise learning — building learning management systems, designing onboarding programs, running certification tracks, and standing up customer and partner education platforms for organizations that couldn't afford to get it wrong.

That work taught us something most technology consultancies never learn firsthand: the hardest part of any transformation isn't the technology. It's the people who have to use it, trust it, and change their daily habits because of it. When generative AI moved from novelty to operating necessity, we didn't pivot away from that expertise — we built our entire firm on top of it.

The Bridge We Built

Two disciplines. Rarely combined. Always necessary.

Discipline One

AI & Workflow Strategy

Process analysis, workflow redesign, automation architecture, and operating-model design grounded in how your business actually runs — not a generic playbook.

Discipline Two

Enterprise Learning & Enablement

Change management, role-based training, custom LMS development, certification design, and adoption strategy built from fifteen years of getting people to change how they work.

Most firms are strong in one of these. Avance was built at the intersection of both — because AI strategy without adoption is just an expensive pilot that quietly dies, and training without a redesigned operating model is activity without impact.
